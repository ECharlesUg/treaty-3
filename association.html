Association

Communities that share in the benefits and obligations of Treaty No. 3 include, Falcon Lake, Middlebro, Point du Bois, etc. Much of Whiteshell Provincial Heritage Park is within the Treaty 3 area as well. Buffalo point first nation was also entered into treaty 3 (Southern Chiefs' Organization Inc).

Treaty #3 existed in the territory of the Anishinaabe Nation, key to Canadian Confederation in that the British wanted to plan a route between Fort Garry and Fort William (now Winnipeg, MB and Thunder Bay, ON respectively) (our-nation).
